  - Estimate: 1 day
  - Priority: 4
  - Status: Incomplete

- US Panel Feedback
  - Description: User will be able to provide text feedback regarding the panel
  - Estimate: 1 day
  - Priority: 4
  - Status: Incomplete
  
- US Game Feedback
